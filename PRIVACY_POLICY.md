# Privacy Policy for StepOne

**Effective Date: October 26, 2023**

StepOne ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application, StepOne.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Physical Activity Data
StepOne collects physical activity data (Step Counts) via the Android Sensor API to provide its core functionality (counting your steps and displaying progress). 
* **Local Storage:** This data is stored locally on your device in a secure database.
* **No Server Upload:** We do NOT upload your step data to our servers or any third-party servers.

### Device Information
We may collect basic device information (model, OS version) via third-party services for debugging and performance monitoring.

## 2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you:

* **Google AdMob:** Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and location data to serve personalized ads. [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* **Razorpay:** Used for optional premium upgrades (Pro Version). Razorpay collects payment information and contact details to process transactions securely. [Razorpay's Privacy Policy](https://razorpay.com/privacy/)

## 3. Data Security
We use `EncryptedSharedPreferences` to store your preferences (like Pro status and Step Goals) securely on your device.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@stepone.com.
